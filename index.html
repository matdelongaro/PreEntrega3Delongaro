ACONCAGUA PARK

This area is internationally known for the highest mountain in the Western Hemisphere, Aconcagua, at 6962 m. It protects the sector of the Andes mountain range where it reaches the highest altitude in America, with numerous peaks that exceed 5,000 m.

SEE MORE
TUPUNGATO PARK

The Tupungato Provincial Park was declared a reserve in 1983. It includes about 15,0000 hectares, protecting an important sector of the Central Andes, but the main objective is the preservation of the Tupungato Volcano, the highest in the Andean massif with its 6,820 meters and its eternal snow and the extensive glaciers that surround it, which feed important Mendoza rivers.
